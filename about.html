About GATT

Global AI Token Tracker — tracking AI inference as commodity, currency, and geopolitical force.

Who

Frank Gao is a tech communications strategist with 15 years of experience at Alibaba, Microsoft, Nokia, and HONOR. GATT started as a personal project to make AI infrastructure data accessible and citable — because the most consequential shift in global compute is happening without a public scoreboard.

Why This Exists

AI inference is the new commodity. Every day, the world's AI systems process hundreds of trillions of tokens — yet there's no Bloomberg Terminal for this data. No standardized index. No one tracking it systematically.

GATT introduces the concept of the Token Economy: the global system of AI inference production, consumption, and exchange. Tokens are the atomic unit of AI work — and they're becoming as economically significant as barrels of oil or kilowatt-hours of electricity.

This project exists to answer simple questions: How many tokens does the world produce per day? Who leads? How fast is it growing? And what does that mean?

Extrapolation Method

When the latest data point is older than 30 days, we extrapolate using compound monthly growth rates derived from at least 2 historical data points. Growth rates are capped at observed maximums and adjusted downward when maturation signals appear (e.g., declining month-over-month acceleration).

Scope: "All Surfaces" vs "API Only"

For vendors like Google who report separate metrics for API usage and total platform usage ("across all surfaces"), GATT uses the All Surfaces scope. This ensures apple-to-apple comparison: ByteDance's figures include all Doubao channels (app + API + internal), and so should Google's.

RatingMeaning
HighDirect company disclosure, ≤3 months old, minimal extrapolation

Very LowEssentially a guess — included for completeness, marked clearly

Dollar-Equivalent Compute (DEC)

Because a token from Doubao costs ~$0.14/T while a token from Claude costs ~$5/T, raw token counts don't tell the full economic story. DEC multiplies daily tokens by blended price-per-trillion to get daily compute value in USD. This reveals that the US leads in compute value despite China leading in token volume.

Data Correction Policy

GATT is an open project. If you're a vendor and our estimate for your service is wrong — we want to hear from you. If you're a researcher with better data — please share it.

Every correction is logged in our Data Changelog with the date, old value, new value, and reason.
